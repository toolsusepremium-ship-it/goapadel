Goa Padel League

Goa's Premier Padel Tournament - Where Competition Meets Community

The Goa Padel League (GPL) marks a significant milestone in the development of padel in India. Featuring 6 franchise teams, the GPL promises a thrilling season of competitive matches, strategic gameplay, and high viewer engagement. The league is designed to promote fairness and provide a platform for both male and female players to showcase their skills.

Game Day Attendance Requirements

On each game day, a minimum of 6 male players and 1 female player must be present to ensure competitive matches.

Additional Team Info

  • Recommended squad: 6-8 players (minimum 2 female)
  • A player may play max 2 rubbers per fixture
  • Mixed Doubles must field 1 male + 1 female

Season Details

Season 1

Season 1 will include 6 teams competing for the championship title.

  • Player Auction: 01/12/2025
  • First Match: 07/01/2026

Game Format

Each game day will feature:

  • 3 Intermediate Men's Doubles Matches
  • 1 Mixed Open Doubles Match
Every match counts towards your team's victory!

Tournament Format

Comprehensive tournament structure designed for maximum excitement and fair competition.

League Stage

  • Round-robin format for the league stage
  • The top 4 teams will advance to the semi-finals
  • 1 final match to determine the champion
Championship glory awaits the best team!

Fixture Structure

Each fixture between two teams consists of 4 matches (rubbers):

  • 1. Men's Intermediate 1
  • 2. Men's Intermediate 2
  • 3. Mixed Doubles 1
  • 4. Men's Intermediate 3
